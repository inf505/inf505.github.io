# Some web games

## Card based

[Scoundrel](https://inf505.github.io/scoundrel/) A classic roguelike adventure played with a standard deck of 52 cards. Outwit monsters, find weapons, and try to survive the dungeon.

[Skeletonking](https://inf505.github.io/skeletonking/) Inspired by Scoundrel, this dark fantasy crawler deepens the experience with persistent gear, unique events, and a world built from shuffled decks of cards.

## LLM Interactive Fiction

[AIMD](https://inf505.github.io/aimd/frontend/) Step into the role of a medical specialist in this LLM-powered diagnostic challenge. Analyze complex patient cases and make critical decisions.

[AIDM](https://inf505.github.io/aidm/frontend/) Forge a unique hero and embark on an unscripted adventure. A state-of-the-art AI acts as your personal Dungeon Master, creating a dynamic story that reacts to your every choice.

[AISF](https://inf505.github.io/aisf/frontend/) In the unforgiving expanse of the settled stars, your story is not pre-written. It is forged in the crucible of choice and consequence. Define your past with a unique Background, specialize your skills with a hardened Archetype, and then step into a universe where every mission, every derelict ship, and every back-alley deal is synthesized in real-time by a state-of-the-art generative AI Game Master. This is not a story you play; it's a saga you build, one desperate contract at a time. Your ship is waiting.
